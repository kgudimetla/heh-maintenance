Understanding the Hybrid Model

Fixed Component (Shared Services)

Services that provide identical value to every doorstep regardless of size: Security, Housekeeping personnel, Garbage collection, and Management technology (MyGate).

UDS Component (Asset Ownership)

Costs related to building capital, common utilities, and legal compliance: Lift AMC, Insurance, DG maintenance, STP/WTP operations, and BESCOM common area charges.
